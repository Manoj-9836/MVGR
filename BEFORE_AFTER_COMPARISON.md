## 🎯 Summary: What Changed

**One Core Principle:** Show, Don't Tell

```
BEFORE: "Enter your sleep hours" (user confused)
AFTER:  "😴 Sleep ℹ️" + examples (user knows what to do)

BEFORE: 6.5 hours (is this good?)
AFTER:  6.5 hours ✅ Perfect (clear feedback)

BEFORE: Settings button hidden
AFTER:  ⚙️ Settings visible + explained

BEFORE: 10 languages listed, all in English
AFTER:  4 languages, all fully translated
```
